# Prayers Loft — TestFlight Tester Instructions

Thanks for testing Prayers Loft — a quiet place to pray, reflect, and remember. Your feedback shapes v1.0.

---

## What to try (in this order)

### 1. First launch (~30 seconds)
- You'll see a 3-slide welcome carousel: **A quiet place to pray** → **Scripture for today** → **Keep your journey**.
- Try tapping **Next**, **Skip**, swiping between slides, and finally **Get Started**.
- *We want to know*: did the onboarding feel calming or pushy? Did it set the right expectation for what the app does?

### 2. Your first prayer (~1 minute)
- On the Prayer tab you'll see suggestion chips like **Peace before a difficult conversation**.
- Tap one OR type anything that's on your heart.
- Tap **Begin**.
- The first time only: a small card appears explaining that prayers are AI-generated. Tap **Continue**.
- A personalized prayer with Scripture appears.
- *We want to know*: did the prayer feel meaningful? Was the AI disclosure too prominent, just right, or unnecessary?

### 3. Scripture (~2 minutes)
- Switch to the **Scripture** tab.
- Read the daily verse and the **Devotional** below it.
- Tap **Bible Questions** (formerly "Theologian") and ask anything — e.g., *"Why does Paul write about suffering as joy?"*
- Tap the share icon to generate a shareable image card.
- *We want to know*: was "Bible Questions" approachable? Did the share card look good enough to actually post?

### 4. Reflections (~2 minutes)
- Switch to **Reflections**. Tap the input and write a few sentences.
- Optionally tap an emotion chip.
- Save it. Notice the streak counter at the top.
- Edit or delete a saved reflection.
- *We want to know*: did the streak feel encouraging or guilting?

### 5. Settings & Account (~1 minute)
- Tap the gear icon top-right.
- Look at the Guest card. Try **Keep My Journey Safe**.
- In the auth sheet: try **Continue with Google** OR **Continue with Email** (create a new account).
- After signing in, return to Settings — the card flips to your signed-in state.
- Try **Sign out** — your local prayers/reflections should remain.
- Try **Delete Account** (it asks for confirmation; your local data is preserved on this device).
- *We want to know*: did Google sign-in feel trustworthy? *(We know it currently shows "Continue to emergentagent.com" — we're aware of this and replacing it before public launch.)*

### 6. The little details
- Try **Settings → Developer Tools → Replay Onboarding** if you want to see the carousel again.
- Force-quit the app and reopen — your data should be exactly where you left it.
- Test on Wi-Fi off if you can — most reads still work from local cache.

## How to report bugs / feedback

In order of preference:
1. TestFlight → *App > Send Beta Feedback* (built-in screenshot + system info)
2. Email to **hello@prayersloft.app** with a screenshot if applicable

What to include for bugs:
- What you tapped
- What you expected
- What you saw
- Device + iOS version
